About Us

GoldenHarvest Poultry is one of Malaysia’s largest vertically integrated poultry enterprises, operating modern hatcheries, feed mills, broiler farms, and processing facilities across Selangor, Johor, and Perak. With over 25 years of experience, we supply premium halal-certified chicken products to supermarkets, restaurants, and export partners across Southeast Asia.

Our Commitment

We uphold strict Malaysian biosecurity standards (DVS Malaysia) and implement advanced environmental controls to ensure healthy livestock and sustainable operations. Our farms operate with automated climate systems, IoT monitoring, and traceability from hatch to delivery.
